14 Most Posh Societies in Bangalore

Bangalore experiences a huge influx of people not just from Karnataka or South India but the entire country. The IT city makes it to the list of cities with highest per capita income in the country. The real estate market in the city is evergreen. The high number of migrant corporate employees are driving the demand for posh societies in Bangalore. 

Below is a list of 14 best societies in Bangalore Central, these societies are sure to offer a high end luxury and comfortable living to all the residents.

Prestige Shantiniketan, one of the posh societies in Bangalore

Prestige Shantiniketan is one of the best gated community apartments in Bangalore, located in Whitefield. It is one of the most sought-after addresses and best societies in Bangalore central and is home to some of the most affluent families. The apartment complex has all the modern amenities that you could want and more. 

From a swimming pool to a fitness centre, a business centre to a spa, Prestige Shantiniketan has it all and can be considered as one of the most Posh societies in Bangalore. The apartment complex is also located near some of the best schools and hospitals in the city, making it the perfect place to live for families with children.

Address: 16, Whitefield Main Rd Prestige Shantiniketan, Palm Meadows, Siddapura, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560048.

Connectivity: The Whitefield Main Road, SH-35, Old Airport Road, and Whitefield Railway Station connect the neighbourhood to the rest of the city. Furthermore, under Phase 2 of Namma Metro, Whitefield is projected to have a metro connection by 2023 with the expansion of the Purple Line from Baiyappanahalli to Whitefield, which would encompass 13 stations. This line would link Whitefield in the east with Kengeri in the west after the expansion construction is completed.
